About us

At Kurre, we strive for excellence in service delivery. This means that we take deep care in where our products originate from.

We try to be as eco-friendly as possible and use only organic Fair trade ® ingredients for our products

As squirrels travel many miles in search of the best snacks, quick bites and energising meals; We too travel around the globe in search of the highest quality products.

We are proud to carry the name Kurre, which is a common nickname for squirrels, as the name for our company.
